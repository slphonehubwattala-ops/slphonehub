Our Story

Founded in 2016 in the heart of Wattala, SL Phone Hub was born from a simple mission: to eliminate the uncertainty of buying premium tech in Sri Lanka.

We recognized that customers wanting the latest iPhone 16 Pro Max deserved more than just a box—they deserved a guarantee of authenticity. Every device we sell, whether "Brand New" or "Certified Pre-Owned," undergoes a rigorous 40-point diagnostic check before it reaches your hands.

Today, we are proud to be Wattala's most trusted marketplace, offering islandwide delivery and 24/7 expert support to over 5,000 happy customers.
